Business Travelers Can Access TripVerify Services to Join
The DHS Traveling Programs Successfully

Have your employees fill in the online application form

Our forms are user-friendly with simplified language to ensure that your employees understand and provide correct information, avoiding applications delays or denials.

Service Charge

Applicants must pay the following fees: TripVerify processing fee: $149 per application DHS Traveling Program Membership: price varies for each of the traveling programs ACSTREK Card fee: one time payment fee valid for 5 years.

Review of your application

Our specialists check for errors, information accuracy and forms completion in a timely manner to assure that your employees applications have every chance of success

Application Form Submission

We create a login.gov account for each of your employees, perform a thorough review of your employee's application, and advise any additional step, documentation or problems they might encounter, before submission.

The Interview Process

Your employees are required to attend an interview in person in order to get approved. We provide assistance throughout the entire process, including the interview. We prepare your employees and advise the documents required to take with them to the interview.

ACSTREK Card

Once your employees' applications are approved, they will receive a unique Known Traveler Number (KTN). Then, your employees will submit their KTN to TripVerify so we can add all their Trusted Traveler Program information in one single card, ACSTREK Card.
